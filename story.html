Verbs

Imagine you're watching a thrilling action movie. The hero is running, jumping, fighting, and saving the day!

Without those action words, the movie would be pretty boring, right? Those words that describe the hero's actions are called verbs.

Verbs are like the superheroes of sentences. They give our language power and energy by expressing an action or a state of being. Without verbs, our sentences would just lie there, lifeless and dull.

In easy words, a verb is a word that indicates a physical action (eg. “swim”), a mental action (eg.“think”), or a state of being (eg.“exist”).
